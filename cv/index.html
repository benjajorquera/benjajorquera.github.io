Publications

  • 2023
    Particle Swarm Optimization of Hyperparameters for Support Vector Machine Regression Models in the Analysis of Cerebral Autoregulation
    University of Santiago de Chile
    Cerebral blood autoregulation is the brain's ability to maintain adequate flow despite changes in blood pressure. There is no standardized protocol for assessing it, leading to various modeling approaches. This study explores using particle swarm optimization (PSO) to improve model selection, comparing it with traditional methods. It focused on FIR and ARX models, both linear and nonlinear. PSO performed well, especially with ARX models, improving search time and yielding results similar to exhaustive searches.
